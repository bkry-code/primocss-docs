Title: Text alignment

----

Text: 

Align your text left, right, centre, justified or inherit from a parent with alignment utility classes. Styles can be found in [_utility.text-align.scss](https://github.com/primocss/primocss/blob/master/scss/_utility.text-align.scss).

######Example
<div class="box box--panel">
<p class="u-align-left">
Left aligned text
</p>
<p class="u-align-center">
Center aligned text
</p>
<p class="u-align-right">
Right aligned text
</p>
</div>

```HTML
<p class="u-align-left">...</p>
<p class="u-align-center">...</p>
<p class="u-align-right">...</p>
```
